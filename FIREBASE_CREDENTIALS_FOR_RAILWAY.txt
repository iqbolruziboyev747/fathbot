# Firebase Credentials ni Railway ga Qo'shish

## Quyidagi qiymatni Railway da FIREBASE_CREDENTIALS variable ga qo'ying:

(PowerShell da quyidagi buyruqni bajaring va chiqadigan qiymatni Railway ga qo'ying)

```powershell
(Get-Content firebase_credentials.json -Raw) -replace "`n", "" -replace "`r", ""
```

Yoki manual ravishda:
1. firebase_credentials.json faylini oching
2. Barcha kontentni nusxalang
3. Railway da FIREBASE_CREDENTIALS variable yarating
4. Qiymat sifatida nusxalangan JSON ni qo'ying
